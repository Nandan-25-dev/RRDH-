Our Academic Programs

Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS)

Duration: 4 Years + 1 Year Internship

The undergraduate dental training program spanning four years of rigorous academic and clinical training, culminating in a one-year compulsory rotatory internship. Affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS) and recognized by the Dental Council of India (DCI).

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB (Min 50%) & NEET Qualification
  • Intake: 100 Seats / Year

Master of Dental Surgery (MDS)

Duration: 3 Years

Specialized postgraduate training offered in various branches of dentistry. Prepares dentists for advanced clinical practice, research, and teaching roles.

Specializations Available:

✔️ Oral Medicine & Radiology
✔️ Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery
✔️ Periodontology
✔️ Conservative Dentistry & Endodontics
✔️ Orthodontics
✔️ Prosthodontics
