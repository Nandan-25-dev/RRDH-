🦷 Dental Health

Common Dental Questions

Quick answers to help patients understand their dental health better.

Visit your dentist at least twice a year (every 6 months) for routine check-ups and cleaning. Early detection prevents major issues.
Call us immediately at 080 – 2848 4210. We provide 24/7 emergency dental services. For knocked-out teeth, keep it moist and come in right away.
Children should have their first dental visit by age 1–2 years, or when the first tooth appears. Early visits build healthy habits and prevent cavities.
Braces treatment typically takes 12 to 24 months depending on the severity of misalignment. Our orthodontists will give a personalised timeline at consultation.
Treatment at RDCH OPD is available at very subsidised rates since the work is done by BDS/MDS students under expert faculty supervision. Call us for fee details.
You can book online using our Appointment Form, or call us at 080 – 2848 4210 during OPD hours (Mon–Sat, 9 AM–5 PM).
