About Rajarajeswari Dental College

Established in 1991, Rajarajeswari Dental College & Hospital has grown into one of Bengaluru's most respected dental institutions. We are recognised by the Dental Council of India and affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences.

Our hospital serves thousands of patients each year across all dental specialities, while also training the next generation of dental professionals through our BDS and MDS programmes.

🎯 Our Vision

To provide world-class dental education and exceptional patient care using cutting-edge technologies, making quality dentistry accessible to all.
